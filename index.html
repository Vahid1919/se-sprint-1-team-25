Welcome to Corona Archive

Using digital tracking to stop the spread of Covid-19

WHICH ONE ARE YOU?

Visitor

Citizens or visitors will use the web service to indicate whether they have entered a particular place and when they have done so.

Places
Places which are frequently visited by people, such as clubs, pubs, restaurants, cinemas etc. will use the web service to get access to a QR code, which uniquely identifies their place. Citizens will scan this QR code to record their presence at that place.
Hospital
The agency or the evaluation client will use the web service to generate corona- related reports by collecting data from the database.
Agency
Hospitals will use the web service to mark people as infected and track anyone else that has been in contact with an infected person.
